Detailed Description

template<class DEST>
class gip::FourierExchange< DEST >

Fourier region exchange.

This transformation reorganizes the quadrants of an images such that region I and III and region II and IV change places respectively. This is normally used to move the 0 frequency pixel to the center of the image for Fourier (and other transformations) space images.

Where the regions are defined as follows:

  • c Region I: is the upper right quadrant.
  • Region II: is the upper left quadrant.
  • Region III: is the lower left quadrant.
  • Region IV: is the lower right quadrant.
