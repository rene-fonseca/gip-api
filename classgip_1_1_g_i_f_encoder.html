Detailed Description

Graphics Interchange Format (GIF) encoder.

Graphics Interchange Format (GIF) encoder/decoder. The Graphics Interchange Format(c) is the Copyright property of CompuServe Incorporated. GIF(sm) is a Service Mark property of CompuServe Incorporated.
